Your privacy matters. Shams al-Asrār does not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to any third party. Data is collected only to provide the service, and you may request deletion at any time.

1. Who We Are

Shams al-Asrār is a horary astrology application developed and operated by Astro Sarfaraz (sole proprietor), based in India. The app is available on the Google Play Store under the package name com.astrosarfaraz.shamsalasrar.

For any privacy-related queries, contact us at: sp6916629@gmail.com

2. Information We Collect

We collect only what is necessary to provide the service:

  • Account information — email address or phone number provided during sign-up via Firebase Authentication (Google Sign-In or email/password).
  • Location data — GPS coordinates at the time you ask a question. Location is used solely to calculate the astronomical chart for that moment (latitude, longitude, and local time). It is not stored permanently or used for tracking.
  • Question content — the text of questions you submit to the oracle, stored in your personal reading history in Firestore.
  • Usage data — question count, daily quota consumption, and subscription status, stored to enforce plan limits.
  • Purchase information — subscription plan, purchase token, and expiry date from Google Play Billing, used to verify and activate your subscription.
  • Device information — anonymised device identifiers used by Firebase for App Check (fraud prevention). We do not collect your full device ID.
  • Crash and diagnostic data — if the app crashes or encounters an error, Firebase Crashlytics records a crash report containing the device model, operating system version, app version, and a technical stack trace. Crash reports do not include your questions, readings, or account details.

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To generate your personalised oracle reading using the astronomical engine.
  • To maintain your reading history within the app.
  • To verify subscription purchases and enforce plan quotas.
  • To authenticate your account securely.
  • To prevent abuse and protect the integrity of the service.
  • To diagnose crashes and fix technical problems in the app.

We do not use your data for advertising, profiling, or sale to third parties.

4. Third-Party Services

The app integrates the following third-party services, each governed by their own privacy policy:

  • Firebase (Google LLC) — Authentication, Firestore database, and Cloud Functions. Data is stored in the asia-south1 (Mumbai) region. Firebase Privacy
  • Google Play Billing (Google LLC) — In-app subscription processing. We receive a purchase token and product ID; full payment details are handled by Google. Google Privacy Policy
  • Firebase Crashlytics (Google LLC) — Crash reporting and stability monitoring. Crash reports are retained by Crashlytics for 90 days. Firebase Privacy
  • Anthropic (Claude API) — The oracle response text is generated using Anthropic's Claude model. Your question text and chart data are sent to Anthropic's API for this purpose. Anthropic Privacy Policy

5. Data Storage and Retention

Your account data and reading history are stored in Firebase Firestore (Mumbai region, India). Local device preferences are stored on-device using encrypted storage and are never transmitted to our servers.

We retain your data for as long as your account is active. If you delete your account, all associated data (readings, quota records, subscription status) is permanently deleted within 30 days.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access — request a copy of the data we hold about you.
  • Correction — request correction of inaccurate data.
  • Deletion — request deletion of your account and all associated data.
  • Portability — request your reading history in a structured format.
  • Withdrawal of consent — you may revoke location permission at any time via your device settings; this will disable chart generation.

To exercise any of these rights, email us at sp6916629@gmail.com. We will respond within 30 days.

7. Children's Privacy

Shams al-Asrār is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal data, please contact us and we will delete it promptly.

8. Security

We use Firebase App Check (Play Integrity API) to ensure only the genuine app can access our backend. All data in transit is encrypted via TLS. Firebase Firestore security rules restrict each user to their own data only.
